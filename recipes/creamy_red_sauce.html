Creamy Tomato Sauce

Description

This creamy tomato pasta sauce is a quick and easy go-to that requires few, cheap ingredients but is extremely flavoursome and perfect for a pasta night at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 30 grams butter 2 tablespoons
  • 1/2 onion finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic finely diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 70 grams tomato paste
  • 3/4 cup cream
  • 40 grams parmesan finely grated
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil and butter in a large frying pan over medium-high heat, and add onions.
  2. Lower to medium-low once sizzling and cook gently for 4-5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and pepper and continue to cook for a minute or until fragrant.
  4. Add the tomato paste and combine, cooking off for 2 minutes to reduce raw taste.
  5. Add in the cream and whisk everything gently to combine. Ensure heat is low so cream does not separate.
  6. Add lemon juice and quickly whisk in.
  7. Add parmesan in stages to allow time to melt into the dish.
  8. Remove from heat and serve with pasta!
